**English Brief**

The ongoing US government shutdown reflects a significant political deadlock, with Democratic leaders taking a hardline stance by withholding salaries from essential federal workers like air traffic controllers and border patrol agents. This has led to extraordinary measures such as a $150 million emergency donation from a wealthy individual to support military personnel impacted by the funding lapse. The shutdown underscores the real-world consequences of partisan gridlock and raises concerns over the functioning of vital government services.

**简体中文摘要**

持续的美国政府停摆反映出严重的政治僵局，民主党领导层采取强硬立场，拒绝向空中交通管制员和边境巡逻队等关键联邦员工发放薪水。这导致一位富豪捐赠1.5亿美元紧急救济受影响的军人。此次停摆凸显党派对立带来的现实影响，并引发对关键政府服务运转的担忧。
