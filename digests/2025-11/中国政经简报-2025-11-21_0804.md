**English Brief**

AI startup Cavela secured $6.6 million in funding to help brands automate supplier sourcing. The founder noted that new tariffs have made customers more cautious about manufacturing products in China, highlighting challenges in the global supply chain. The company aims to use technology to reduce tariff impact and optimize cost structure.

**简体中文摘要**

人工智能初创公司Cavela获得660万美元融资，帮助品牌自动化供应商采购流程。创始人指出，新增关税促使客户对在中国制造产品更加谨慎，凸显了全球供应链面临的挑战。该公司致力于通过技术降低关税影响，优化成本结构。
